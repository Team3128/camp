Registration Policies

The following details our policies regarding the registration process:

Registration Deadline

Registration and all payments are due before the first day of the week you are registering for. If you register by April 15th, you are eligble for a 10% early bird discount! The discount will be automatically applied at checkout.

Pay Online

The online registration forms are linked to on the individual program information pages, linked above.

Refunds

Information regarding refunds will be available soon. Refunds are issued through the Canyon Crest Academy Foundation.

Wait List

We allot a maximum of 16 slots per program per week. When a camp week has become full, campers may join a wait list. If a slot becomes available, campers on the wait list will be contacted in the order they registered and given a reasonable amount of time to respond and claim their spot.
