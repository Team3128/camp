COVID-19 Impact

How COVID-19 Will Affect Camp

We recognize that the recent spread of the novel coronavirus has raised many concerns over the past couple of weeks and has led to many closures nationwide. The safety and health of our campers and counselors is our top priority, and due to the implemented county and school guidelines, the 2020 Robotics Summer Camp is canceled. If you have any questions regarding this new update, please do not hesitate to contact at us at camp@team3128.org.

Cancellations & Refunds

Please refer to the following policies regarding cancellations and refunds.

Cancellations

Due to the current COVID-19 situation, unfortunately Robotics Camp for the 2020 Summer is fully canceled. If you have previously registered for the camp, you should have received an email regarding this update. We are currently figuring out how refunding is going to work. We thank you for your patience as we plan the appropriate next steps. More updates coming soon.

Refunds

Regarding refunds, at this point in time, our advisors are going to be in contact with those who have already registered for the camp, and are currently working to find the best plan for reimbursement. We will update this with new information soon.
